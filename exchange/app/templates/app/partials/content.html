Main features of Djongo-Exchange

Responsive

Django-Exchange is a responsive platform where dynamic contents change, images are resized and spaces take the right shape according to needs.

The platform at the time of registration gives each user a random amount of bitcoins ranging from 1 to 10

MongoDB

MongoDB is a non-relational, document-oriented DBMS that offers scalability and flexibility

Free

The platform in question is totally free for users and does not withhold any type of commission on operations.
